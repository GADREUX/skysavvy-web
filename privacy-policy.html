SkySavvy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SkySavvy Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google ID to create and identify your account.
  • Authentication Token: A secure token issued by Google is stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
  • Flight Watch Data: The flight routes, dates, and alert preferences you configure are stored to enable price monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account via Google Sign-In.
  • To monitor flight prices for your saved routes.
  • To send you notifications when prices drop or rise on your monitored routes.
  • To display your flight watches and price history within the extension.

3. Data Storage

Your authentication token and flight watches are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. Flight watch data is also stored on our secure backend server to enable background price monitoring.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core flight price tracking functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

SkySavvy uses the following third-party services solely to provide its functionality:

  • Google OAuth: For user authentication.
  • Flight data APIs: To fetch real-time flight prices (no personal data is shared with these services).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: To deliver push notifications to your device.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by uninstalling the extension and contacting us at the email below.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between the extension and our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

SkySavvy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
